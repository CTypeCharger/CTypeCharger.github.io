Hi, my name is Yeonwoo Choi. I am a Combined UG–G (Integrated B.S.–M.S.–Ph.D.) student at DGIST (Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology), South Korea. I work on ROS 2, middleware, and networked robotic systems — especially how discovery and discovery traffic behave when many robots share a wireless network.

Research interests

  • ROS 2 nodes, executors, and deployment on real hardware
  • DDS / Zenoh and scalability when the number of participants or topics grows
  • Wireless robotic networks — latency, loss, and discovery storms at scale
