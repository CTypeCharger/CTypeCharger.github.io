Hi, my name is **Yeonwoo Choi**. I am a Combined UG–G (Integrated B.S.–M.S.–Ph.D.) student at [DGIST](https://www.dgist.ac.kr/en/) (Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology), South Korea. I work on **ROS 2**, **middleware**, and **networked robotic systems** — especially how discovery and discovery traffic behave when many robots share a wireless network.

I am most comfortable building and experimenting with **ROS 2** and **DDS**-family middleware (including **Zenoh** where it meets our lab’s networking questions).

## Research interests

* **ROS 2** nodes, executors, and deployment on real hardware  
* **DDS / Zenoh** and scalability when the number of participants or topics grows  
* **Wireless robotic networks** — latency, loss, and discovery storms at scale  

## Selected publication
